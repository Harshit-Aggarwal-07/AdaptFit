# Brand Style

## Product Name

Adaptive Motion Gym

## Tagline

Your pace. Your ability. Your movement.

## Visual Direction

The UI should feel:

- modern
- warm
- inclusive
- premium
- accessible
- calming but energetic
- hackathon-demo ready

It should not feel:

- clinical
- boring
- shame-based
- childish
- like a plain form

## Suggested Colors

- Background: warm off-white, soft cream, or very light lavender
- Primary: teal or emerald
- Secondary: indigo or soft purple
- Text: deep navy or charcoal
- Warning/safety: warm amber
- Success: accessible green

## UI Elements

- Rounded cards
- Soft shadows
- Large readable typography
- Friendly icons
- Progress chips
- Inclusive illustrations
- Clear step indicators
- Polished completion screen
- Accessible contrast

## Copy Style

Use:

- Move with confidence.
- Adapted for your body.
- Your pace, your ability.
- Strength in every form.
- Every body is welcome.
