## Demo Script

1. Open Adaptive Motion Gym.
2. Select “Wheelchair Strength Demo.”
3. Generate adaptive routine.
4. Notice all exercises are seated/wheelchair-friendly.
5. Click “This does not work for me” on one exercise.
6. Watch the app replace it.
7. Start workout session.
8. Enable voice guidance or high contrast.
9. Complete workout.
10. View Confidence Points, badges, muscles targeted, and feedback summary.

## Safety Disclaimer

Adaptive Motion Gym provides general inclusive movement suggestions. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, medically prescribe, or replace professional care. Stop if you feel pain, dizziness, numbness, or discomfort.
