# Adaptive Motion Gym Product Brief

## Product Name

Adaptive Motion Gym

## Tagline

Your pace. Your ability. Your movement.

## Vision

Adaptive Motion Gym is an inclusive AI-powered movement companion for people who are often excluded by traditional fitness apps.

The product creates adaptive, safety-aware workout routines for users with different mobility levels, limb abilities, support needs, pain areas, body types, confidence levels, and accessibility preferences.

The goal is not shame-based transformation. The goal is movement confidence, comfort, consistency, and strength in every form.

## Core Principle

Fitness should adapt to the person, not force the person to adapt to standard fitness.

## Target Users

- Wheelchair users
- People with limb differences
- People with limited arm or leg function
- People using prosthetics
- People with low balance confidence
- People with pain or injury areas
- People recovering from injury
- Veterans or army personnel with physical limitations
- Overweight or underweight users
- Very short or very tall users
- Users with low fitness confidence
- Users needing seated, supported, low-impact, or adapted movement
- Users needing accessibility support

## Product Pillars

1. Ability Profile
2. Adaptive workout generation
3. Safety validation
4. Exercise replacement
5. Voice-guided accessible workout mode
6. Optional Motion Check
7. Adaptive Motion Sketch
8. Post-workout feedback
9. Confidence-based progress
10. Inclusive badges
11. Accessibility-first UX

## Safety Positioning

This product provides general inclusive movement suggestions. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, medically prescribe, or replace professional care.

Users should stop if they feel pain, dizziness, numbness, or discomfort.
