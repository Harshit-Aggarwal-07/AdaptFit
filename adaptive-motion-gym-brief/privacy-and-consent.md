# Privacy and Consent Guidelines

Adaptive Motion Gym may collect sensitive movement, accessibility, and body-related information.

## Principles

- Collect only what is needed for personalization.
- Prefer functional questions over medical diagnosis questions.
- Explain why data is collected.
- Let users update or reset their Ability Profile.
- Make image upload optional.
- Make camera/Motion Check optional.
- Do not permanently store images, frames, or videos unless explicitly approved.
- For demo mode, local/session storage is acceptable.
- Do not infer sensitive identity traits from images.
- Do not judge body appearance.

## Suggested User Copy

> Your Ability Profile is used to personalize adaptive movement suggestions. You can update or reset it anytime.

> Camera-based Motion Check is optional. It provides general movement feedback, not medical assessment.

> You can skip image and camera features and still use Adaptive Motion Gym.

## Do Not Say

- We medically analyze your body.
- We diagnose your posture.
- We store your body scan permanently.
- We guarantee safe exercise.
