## 🩺 The Problem

Physiotherapy and adaptive fitness are among the most **access-gated** forms of healthcare:

- Rehab clinics are expensive, geographically limited, and hard to reach for people with mobility constraints, disabilities, or in rural/underserved regions.
- Adaptive exercise programs — designed for different body types, injuries, and ability levels — largely don't exist outside specialized (and costly) clinical settings.
- Without real-time feedback, at-home exercise is either unsafe (wrong form → injury) or ineffective (no correction → no progress).
- Millions of people abandon prescribed exercise/rehab routines simply because there's no one watching, correcting, or encouraging them.

**The result:** the people who need consistent, correctly-guided movement the most are the least likely to get it.

## 💡 Our Solution

**AdaptiFit** is a real-time, AI-powered adaptive coaching platform that builds a **3D digital twin** of your body, tests and calibrates exercises to *your* specific physical profile, and then guides you live through **visual and voice feedback** as you move — like having a physiotherapist watching over your shoulder, available anytime, on any device with a camera.

We designed AdaptiFit specifically for **inclusivity**: the system adapts to different body shapes, mobility ranges, and ability levels, instead of assuming a single "default" body — which is how most fitness tech is built today.

## ✨ Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🧍 **3D Digital Twin** | Builds a personalized 3D model of the user's body and range of motion, used as the baseline for all future exercise calibration. |
| 🎯 **Adaptive Exercise Calibration** | Tests how the user's body moves and adapts exercise plans/thresholds to their individual mobility, injury constraints, and ability level — not a one-size-fits-all program. |
| 📹 **Real-Time Motion Monitoring** | Live pose tracking while the user exercises, comparing actual movement against the calibrated, personalized form in real time. |
| 🗣️ **Voice + Visual Guided Feedback** | Corrective cues delivered simultaneously as on-screen visual overlays and spoken voice guidance, so feedback is accessible regardless of visual or hearing preference. |
| ♿ **Inclusivity by Design** | Built to accommodate diverse body types, disabilities, and mobility ranges rather than a single "ideal" reference posture. |
| 🌍 **Accessible Anywhere** | Runs with just a camera-equipped device — no clinic, no specialized hardware, no appointment required. |

## ⚙️ How It Works

```
1. Calibrate  →  Capture body geometry & range of motion → build 3D digital twin
2. Adapt      →  Generate/adjust exercise thresholds personalized to the user's twin
3. Move       →  User performs the exercise in front of a camera
4. Monitor    →  Real-time pose estimation tracks joints/limbs against the calibrated form
5. Guide      →  Deviations trigger instant visual overlays + spoken voice corrections
6. Progress   →  Session data feeds back into the digital twin to refine future guidance
```

## 🌍 Why This Matters (Impact & Inclusivity)

- **Removes the "right room" barrier** — rural, mobility-limited, and underserved users get access to adaptive coaching that today only exists in specialized clinics.
- **Personalizes, rather than standardizes** — most fitness/rehab tech assumes an "average" body; AdaptiFit's digital twin approach explicitly adapts to different bodies and abilities.
- **Multi-modal feedback** — pairing visual and voice guidance makes the experience more accessible across different needs and preferences.
- **Scales physiotherapist-quality attention** — real-time correction without requiring a 1:1 human session for every user, every time.

## ⚠️ Safety & Medical Disclaimer

AdaptiFit provides general inclusive movement and wellbeing guidance only. It does **not** diagnose, treat, cure, or replace professional medical care, and is **not** a substitute for a clinician or physical therapist.

**Stop immediately if you feel pain, dizziness, numbness, or discomfort, and consult a qualified professional for medical, injury, or rehabilitation needs.**

If you're in crisis, call or text the **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline**.
